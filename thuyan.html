Library Exhibit

The Library seeks to provide an opportunity for artists, collectors, and organizations to exhibit their works to the community. Local artists are encouraged to apply for an exhibit at least 3 months in advance of their proposed start date. All exhibitions will be free and open to the public. All exhibit applications go through the Library Advisory Board for approval. Please see the orange
Library Exhibit Policy.
